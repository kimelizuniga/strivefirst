About Us

Strive First began off as a Student's Moving Service company, which was owned and operated by Yves Burume comprised of a network of students and graduates from Colleges, Universities, Community colleges and high schools.

The name was later changed to Strive First as the companies ambition grew.

Their passion is dedicated to providing work ethic and experience to students and those in need. Strive First is focused on supporting clients in Moving, Installations, assembly services primary. Our mission is to provide a team of experienced professionals to support the community in the most convenient and effective way possible. Our objective is to assist our clients to regain and maintain cashflow independence, save them time and help them manage pain.
